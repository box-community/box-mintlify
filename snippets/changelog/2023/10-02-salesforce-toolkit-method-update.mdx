## Salesforce Toolkit - Integration Mappings

  You can now use [integration mappings][2023-10-02_1] in Salesforce Toolkit with new [methods][2023-10-02_2] and [flow actions][2023-10-02_3].
With [integration mappings][2023-10-02_4] you can map a Slack channel to any folder within your enterprise instead of the default one.
